    • FAQ

    Q. When is the event held?

    A. The photo contest is held every month as a way to encourage photographers of all levels to continuously challenge themselves and improve their craft. By offering a new theme or prompt each month, participants are given a chance to experiment with different subjects and techniques, and to push their creativity to new heights.

    Q. Can i only have 1 vote?

    A. No, you can vote for more than one entries. Allowing voters to choose more than one entry in a photo contest helps to promote a more inclusive and diverse voting process. By allowing voters to vote for multiple entries, it ensures that participants who may have multiple strong submissions are not unfairly disadvantaged by being limited to only one vote.

    Q. Would I see my rankings after the award?

    A. No, there is no ranking in this contest. Winners of the contest will be announced. The contest winners will be chosen based on their individual merits, without being assigned any specific numerical or ranked value.

    Q. Who are the judges?

    A. The judges are the public or everyone who visits this website, everyone has the right to vote even the contestant.

    Q. Will I know whose picture of every entry?

    A. No, because you can only judge a PICTURE not the details. We will endeavor to safeguard the privacy of the photographer to the fullest extent possible.

    Q. Do i need to make an account to vote?

    A. No, You are not required to login or signup account to make a vote. The voting system is for the whole public. You are only required to register or fill up the form to join the contest.

    Q. Are there any qualifications required to enter the contest?

    A. You can enter the contest regardless of nationality, age, gender, or professional/amateur status. Individuals 18 years of age or younger must have consent from a guardian. We will judge at the time of entry that guardian consent has been received.
