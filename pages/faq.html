    • FAQ

    Q. Who are the judges?

    A. The judges are the public or everyone who visit this website, everyone is has right to vote even the contestant.

    Q. Would i see my rankings after the awardings?

    A. No, there is no ranking on this contest. Winners of the contest will be announce.

    Q. Can i see the details of every pics of every entry?

    A. No, because you can only judge a PICTURE not the details.

    Q. Can i only have 1 vote?

    A. No, you can vote more than one.

    Q. When is the event held?

    A. Once every two year.

    Q. Do i need to make an account to vote?

    A. No, You are not required to login or signup account to make a vote. The voting system is for the whole public. You are only required to make an account to join the contest.

    Q. Are there any qualifications required to enter the contest?

    A. You can enter the contest regardless of nationality, age, gender, or professional/amateur status. Individuals 18 years of age or younger must have consent from a guardian. We will judge at the time of entry that guardian consent has been received.
