    • FAQ

    Q. Are there any qualifications required to enter the contest?

    A. You can enter the contest regardless of nationality, age, gender, or professional/amateur status. Individuals 18 years of age or younger must have consent from a guardian. We will judge at the time of entry that guardian consent has been received.

    Q. Is there any fee to enter the contest?

    A. There is no fee to participate. However, you are responsible for costs associated with the entry such as the internet connection fee.

    Q. Is entry limited to works taken with Nikon photography equipment?

    A. You may use any photography or film equipment.

    Q. Will submitted works be returned?

    A. No, submitted works will not be returned.
